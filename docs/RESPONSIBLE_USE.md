# Responsible Use

## What This Product Is For

This product is designed to help students plan, understand, research, draft, and check academic work — as a guide alongside their own thinking, not a replacement for it. It's built for the moments where a student knows what they're trying to do but needs help getting there: structuring an argument, finding credible sources, understanding a difficult concept, or catching a weak spot in their own reasoning before it becomes a problem.

## What This Product Is Not For

This product is not designed to produce final, submission-ready work with no student involvement, to help represent AI-generated content as entirely the student's own unaided work, or to help a student get around an assignment's actual purpose. Using it this way isn't just against the spirit of the product — it also defeats the reason it exists. A student who skips the thinking doesn't get the benefit the product is built to provide.

## Guidance for Students

**Use outputs as a starting point.** Research, drafts, and summaries are meant to be reviewed, questioned, and rewritten in your own voice — not copied in directly.

**Check the sources, don't just trust the claim.** Every research-backed skill shows you where information came from. Use that. Building the habit of checking sources is part of what the product is trying to teach.

**Know your institution's policies.** Academic integrity rules around AI use vary by school, course, and even individual assignment. This product doesn't set those rules and can't guarantee its use complies with any specific policy — that responsibility sits with the student. When in doubt, ask your instructor before using AI assistance on a given assignment.

**Use the checking skills on your own work, honestly.** Skills like `review`, `flag`, and `verify` are most valuable when used to genuinely improve your understanding and your draft — not to polish something you don't actually understand well enough to defend.

## A Note on Limits

No set of product boundaries can guarantee responsible use on its own. This document is guidance, not enforcement — the product is built to make the honest path the easiest one, but the choice of how to use it ultimately belongs to the student.
